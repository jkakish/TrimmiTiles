Product Overview

Trimmi Tiles is the first comprehensive health solution for dairy cattle, integrating a sophisticated RFID sensor system that detects early signs of Gait's disease. Coupled with a proprietary hoof trimming mat, it ensures the gradual wearing down of hooves to prevent complications, thereby enhancing overall cattle well-being and productivity.

Each modular tile measures 1' x 1' and is priced at $499.99. Our durable trimming mats are available in 10' wide rolls for $49.99, designed for annual replacement to maintain optimal condition.
